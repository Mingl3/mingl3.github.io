The First Exclusive College Dating App

Tired of endlessly swiping to find someone you are interested in?


Mingl allows students to customize their online dating experience, offering a simple, yet rich interface from which they can meet others. Allowing you to sort by major, school, personality, and many other details, Mingl is the app you have been looking for.


Our Story

We are a bunch of college students from University of Michigan and Cornell University hoping to disrupt the dating app space for college students.
